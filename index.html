About Us

At Put-in Designs, we believe that your space should be a reflection of your personality, values, and lifestyle. Our mission is simple: to bring your space to life with tailor-made carpentry solutions that are both functional and visually stunning. Founded by Bongani Mazibuko, a passionate interior designer with a keen eye for detail, Put-in Designs specializes in creating custom kitchens, wardrobes, TV units, bar units, and walk-in closets. Each project is crafted with the highest level of precision, using top-quality materials to ensure durability and elegance. Our approach is customer-centric, meaning we listen carefully to your needs, offering creative designs that perfectly match your vision. Whether you're redesigning your kitchen or creating a new living room unit, we handle everything from design to installation, ensuring seamless execution and outstanding results. At Put-in Designs, we don’t just design spaces—we create environments that inspire. Every project, big or small, is a testament to our commitment to excellence, innovation, and your satisfaction. Let us transform your space with designs that are tailored just for you. We’re here to help you make your dream a reality. Get in touch with us today to bring your vision to life.
